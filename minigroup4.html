Colorado State University

Atmospheric Modeling

Research Focus

The Atmospheric Modeling sub-group uses a variety of numerical modeling tools to investigate our questions of interest. These include the transition of weak vorticity anomalies in easterly waves to mature tropical cyclones, the interaction between tropical cyclones and the overturning tropical circulation, and the tropical cloud feedback processes.

The members of the TC-RAMS research group that are part of the Atmospheric Modeling minigroup are: Tyler Barbero, Delián Colón Burgos, Chandler Jenkins, Meghan Stell, and Levi Silvers (lead)
